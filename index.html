Mohit’s Motors is an online store that ships a variety of car parts to people all around the world. We sell suspension kits, superchargers, tires, rims, body kits and brake pads. The business was founded by Mohit Amarthaluru, and he is helped by two others to help ship all the car parts to meet your needs! From our warehouse in London, Ontario we provide free worldwide shipping. Also, when you order from us you can be assured that you will get the best deal possible.
